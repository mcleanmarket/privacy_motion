Privacy Policy

Who We Are

McLean Marketing LLC (doing business as Motion Marketing) is a marketing and sales company based in Provo, Utah. We operate lead generation campaigns on behalf of internet service providers in select markets.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when interacting with our advertisements and lead forms.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form through one of our advertisements or landing pages, we may collect the following:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home or service address
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

We may also collect basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, and referring URL through standard web analytics.

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit is used solely for the purpose of determining internet service availability at your address and connecting you with a service representative. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Verify service eligibility at your location
  • Contact you by phone, text message, and/or email regarding internet service options
  • Schedule appointments or follow-up calls
  • Pass your information to the applicable internet service provider for fulfillment

By submitting your information, you expressly consent to being contacted by phone call, SMS/text message, and email by Motion Marketing and/or its service provider partners. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message or contacting us directly.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Internet service providers for the purpose of fulfilling your service inquiry
  • Technology platforms (such as CRM and marketing tools) used to manage communications

We require any third parties who receive your data to handle it in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. If you wish to have your information removed from our records, please contact us using the information below.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Cookies & Tracking

Our pages and advertising campaigns may use cookies and pixels (including the Meta Pixel) to measure ad performance and optimize delivery. These tools collect anonymized data about page visits and form submissions. They do not collect payment information.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or opt out of Meta ad tracking via your Facebook account settings.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the most recent changes were made. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
